Beard Restoration Process

Our specialized approach ensures facial symmetry and direction-perfect hair growth for a seamless look.

Design & Planning

We work with you to design a beard line that complements your facial features, correcting patchiness and defining the cheek and jawline.

Donor Extraction

Hair follicles are carefully selected from the back of the scalp (donor area) to match the texture and angle of your facial hair.

Precision Implantation

Follicles are implanted at precise angles and depth to mimic natural beard growth patterns, ensuring high density and flow.

Recovery & Growth

Minimal downtime with tiny scabs healing in a week. New beard hair starts growing in 3-4 months and fully matures in a year.

Who is a good candidate for beard transplant?

Men with patchy beards, facial scarring, or sparse facial hair due to genetics are ideal candidates. You must have sufficient donor hair on your scalp.

How long does the procedure take?

A beard transplant typically takes 3 to 6 hours, depending on the area to be covered and the number of grafts required for desired density.

When can I shave my new beard?

You can gently trim your beard after 2 weeks once the scabs have fallen off. Normal shaving can usually resume after 3-4 months when the skin has fully healed.

Will the results look natural?

Absolutely. We carefully angle and place each graft to match your natural growth pattern. Once grown, it is indistinguishable from a natural beard.

How much does a beard transplant cost?

The cost depends on the number of grafts needed. We offer competitive pricing and flexible EMI plans. Book a consultation for a precise quote.
