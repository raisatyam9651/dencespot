Laser Hair Removal Process

Our step-by-step approach ensures a safe experience and maximum effectiveness for lasting smooth skin.

Consultation & Patch Test

We analyze your skin type and hair texture to customize laser settings. A small patch test ensures safety and compatibility.

Shaving & Preparation

The target area is shaved to allow the laser energy to penetrate directly into the hair follicle roots without surface burns.

Laser Application

Precise laser pulses target melanin in hair follicles, heating and destroying them to inhibit future growth, while cooling protects the skin.

Post-Care & Protection

A soothing gel is applied post-treatment. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure comprehensive skin protection.

Is laser hair removal permanent?

Laser hair removal offers permanent hair reduction. While most hair follicles are destroyed, maintenance sessions might be required once a year to catch any new growth triggered by hormonal changes.

How many sessions do I need?

Generally, 6 to 8 sessions are recommended for optimal results. This varies depending on your hair thickness, skin type, and the hormonal balance of your body.

Is the procedure painful?

Most clients describe the sensation as a light rubber band snap. Our advanced diode laser technology features a built-in cooling system to minimize discomfort and protect the skin.

Can I shave between sessions?

Yes, shaving is permitted and even recommended before your session. However, you should avoid waxing, plucking, or threading as the laser needs the hair root to be present to work effectively.

Are there any side effects?

Laser hair removal is safe when performed by experts. Temporary redness or mild swelling may occur immediately after the session but typically subsides within a few hours.
